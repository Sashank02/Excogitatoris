What do we do ?

Good health is a precious thing. When we are healthy we can learn, work, and support ourselves and our families. When we are sick, we struggle, and our families and communities fall behind.

Together, we will deliver the promise of medicine. The mission of "Health Care" is to improve the health of the community and the world by setting the standard of excellence in medical education, research and clinical care.

Our Vision

"Health Care" has been on a path to create a healthy culture for all. The "Health Care" describes our shared culture and how we aspire to treat each other, our patients and the communities we serve.

  • Our vision: To make India one of the World’s healthiest Country.
  • Our promise: The Best Care, Designed for You
  • Our values:
    • Purpose: We work to do good in the lives of all others
    • Excellence: We do our best at all times and in new ways
    • Compassion: We treat all people with respect, kindness and empathy
    • Team: We count on and care for each other
